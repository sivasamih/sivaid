SIVA-ID is a sub-division of SIVA Group. At SIVA-ID we have developed a range of tags and labels to suit heavy industries or challenging environments. We operate globally to deliver high-quality products at competitive rates.

THE TEAM


Rajiv K. Aggarwal

Founder & CEO

Founded SIVA in 1986 and made it a major player in the Tag, Labels & Ticketing Industry. With over 30 years of in-depth experience in New Product & Business Development, hands on with machines & tooling, knowledge of materials & processes. A true inspiration and leader of innovation at Siva Group.

Arjun Aggarwal

COO & Board Member

With over 12 years of experience working on products for different industries, Arjun spearheads Sales and is also involved in Group strategy formulation, new market developments, product management & overall operations.

Neil Dunn

VP - Industrial Tags & Labels

From leaving College Neil has been in and around Steel Mills starting his career as an engineer fixing label printers. Now with 25 years of experience within the Steel, Metals, Construction and Chemicals Industries. Joins Siva Group in 2022 to lead our new Industrial Tags and Labels Division.
